During lunch you check your phone and see your name in a big group chat. Someone shared an embarrassing photo of you from gym class without your permission. People are laughing and calling you names in the thread. Your face feels hot and you want to disappear.

What are you going to do?

Saving evidence and telling a trusted adult is a brave way to handle cyberbullying. Adults can help get the photo removed and support you without making things worse.

Fighting back online can escalate the situation and get you in trouble too. Cyberbullying is never your fault — getting help is the stronger choice.
