After school you open your phone and scroll through your class feed. Jorge, a classmate who moved in this year, posted a picture from the science fair. In the comments, Trevor and a few older students are making cruel jokes about Jorge's accent and telling people not to be friends with him. Jorge hasn't replied, but his last post makes it clear he is upset.

What are you going to do?

Reporting cyberbullying helps protect Jorge and shows him he is not alone. A trusted adult can address the harm and help stop it from spreading online.

Ignoring mean comments lets bullies think their behavior is okay. Jorge might feel even more isolated when no one speaks up for him.
